We provide

holistic care and support to the girl child in UGANDA

Education

We ensure every girl child gets quality primary and secondary education. We cover school fees, uniforms, books, and provide after-school tutoring.

Healthcare

Regular medical checkups, vaccinations, and emergency care. We also provide nutritious meals daily and teach hygiene and health practices.

Shelter

A safe, loving home full of smiles with dedicated house parents. We provide beds, clothing, and a family environment where every girl child feels protected and valued.

MISSION, VISION AND SLOGAN


MISSION

To nurture, educate, and empower every girl child by providing shelter, food, healthcare, and quality education in a family environment..

VISION

A world where every girl child has the opportunity to grow up in a loving home full of smiles and reach their full potential.
