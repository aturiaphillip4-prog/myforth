ABOUT US

Giving a smile to a girl child.

RAISING GIRLS

RAISING GIRLS is a home dedicated to providing love, care, and support to girl children in Kampala, Uganda. Since our founding, we have worked to create a safe environment where every child feels protected and valued.

Our Mission

To nurture, educate, and empower every girl child by providing shelter, food, healthcare, and quality education in a family environment.

Our Vision

A world where every girl child has the opportunity to grow up in a loving home full of smiles and reach their full potential.

Shelter

A safe, loving home full of smiles with dedicated house parents. We provide beds, clothing, and a family environment where the girl child feels protected and valued.

Education

Quality education for every girl child. We support school fees, books, uniforms, and extra tutoring to ensure no girl is left behind.

Healthcare

Regular medical check-ups, nutrition, and counseling. We prioritize both physical and emotional well-being for every child in our care.
